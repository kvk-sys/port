The Best Movies According to KVK

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Avatar

The 2009 movie starring Sam Worthington, Zoe Saldana, and Sigourney Weaver is about a paraplegic Marine who is sent to the moon Pandora on a mission. It has a lifetime gross of $2,923,706,026.

Avengers: Endgame

The 2019 movie starring Robert Downey Jr., Chris Evans, and Mark Ruffalo is about the Avengers reassembling to restore balance to the universe after the events of Avengers: Infinity War. It has a lifetime gross of $2,799,439,100.

Avatar: The Way of Water

he 2022 movie starring Sam Worthington, Zoe Saldana, and Sigourney Weaver is about Jake Sully working with Neytiri and the Na'vi to protect their home from a familiar threat. It has a lifetime gross of $2,320,250,281.
